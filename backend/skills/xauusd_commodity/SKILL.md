# XAUUSD Commodity Protocol Skill

## When This Skill Is Active
Any analysis involving XAUUSD_PERP (Gold perpetual futures).
Gold behaves fundamentally differently from crypto — different sessions,
different correlations, different liquidity profile.

## Critical Difference: Gold Is NOT Crypto
Gold markets are driven by USD strength/weakness (DXY correlation: -0.7 to -0.9),
US interest rate expectations, and institutional flows (central banks, ETFs).
The funding rate on XAUUSD perpetuals reflects crypto market mechanics applied
to a non-crypto asset — use with extreme caution.

## Trading Session Protocol (CRITICAL)

### AVOID — Asia Session (00:00-13:00 IST)
Liquidity extremely thin. Most "breakouts" during Asia session reverse in London.
Exception: significant macro news from Asia/Middle East.

### CAUTION — Pre-London (13:00-14:00 IST)
Watch for stop hunts before the real move. Observe only.

### PRIMARY WINDOW — London Open (14:00-18:30 IST)
Highest quality gold setups. London institutional flows set the day's direction.
OBs formed in London session are highly reliable. Take gold trades here.

### SECONDARY WINDOW — London/NY Overlap (18:30-22:30 IST)
High volatility, follow-through on London direction.
Be cautious of reversals at 21:00 IST (3:30pm NY) as US session slows.

### AVOID — Late US/Asia transition (22:30-00:00 IST)
Do not open new gold positions.

## DXY Correlation Rule
Before any XAUUSD trade: USD strengthening + going LONG gold = fighting the trend.
USD weakening + going LONG gold = with the trend.

## XAUUSD Setup Adjustments
- OBs must form during London or NY session (Asia OBs unreliable)
- The liquidity sweep on 1H must be at least 0.3% in size
- Avoid entries at exact round numbers ($2,300, $2,350 etc.) — central banks defend these

## R:R Consideration
Gold's typical daily range: 0.3%–0.8% (much less than BTC's 2-5%).
Set TP targets proportionally smaller — do NOT expect 2% moves from gold setups.
